Here's your script: What's the deal with stocks? Think of it like owning a tiny piece of your favorite coffee shop! You buy shares and become part-owner. Simple idea, huge impact! Imagine you put $100 in Apple back when they were just getting started. Today that $100 would be worth thousands! That's the power of compound interest, folks. So, what are stocks? Stocks represent ownership in a company. When you invest, you're buying tiny pieces of those companies' future successes. It's like betting on your favorite team to win – but instead of a game, it's the market! The goal is to buy low and sell high – or hold tight for long-term gains!
